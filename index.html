Biography

Heqian Qiu is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof.H.Li. in Intelligent Vision Information Processing Laboratory, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. Before that, She received her B.Sc. degree in electronic information science and technology from Shanxi Datong University in 2015.

Her research interests include computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on object detection, referring expression comprehension and vision-language. She has served as a reviewer for IEEE T-CSVT, JVCI, AAAI and ECCV.
